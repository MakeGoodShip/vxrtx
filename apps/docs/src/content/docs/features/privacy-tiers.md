## Three Tiers

### Secure
Fully local AI. Your data never leaves your machine.

- **Chrome Built-in AI** (Gemini Nano) — zero setup, runs inside Chrome
- **Ollama** — connect to a local model running on your machine

### Relaxed
Cloud AI with minimal data exposure.

- Only tab/bookmark **titles** are sent — no URLs
- Works with OpenRouter, Claude, or OpenAI

### YOLO
Full context sent to AI for the best possible results.

- Titles, URLs, and timestamps
- Produces the most accurate groupings
- Same provider options as Relaxed
